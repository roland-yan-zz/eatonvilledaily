May Mayor Starks Rest In Peace.

An obituary and memorial tribute to our fallen Mayor.

Posted by The Starks Family on April 4, 1889

A Great Loss.

Our community sadly lost a great pillar last Friday, the 2nd of April, when Mayor Jody Starks, affectionately known Joe, died of natural causes.

Mayor Starks was a great man, a devoted husband, and a dedicated mayor. Under his leadership, he turned a collection of houses into one of the most prominent towns of Florida that we are proud to call our home.

He made American history as the first mayor of an all black town, established the first black post office, and installed the lights of Eatonville.

Our Mayor is survived by his legacy: by all the people he touched, all the lives he improved, and all the work he did for the town. At this time, the family is inviting every citizen of Orange County to the funeral of Mayor Jody Starks

Help his kindness, selflessness, and graciousness be remembered by making a donation to the Eatonville Planning committee so we may erect a statue in the Mayor’s honor.

May He Rest In Eternal Peace.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
