Eatonville’s First Post Office!

Earlier this week, Eatonville welcomed the creation of its very first Post Office, ushered in by our new Mayor, Joe Starks.

The Post Office is a directly-related governing entity, and as a result, validates our position as an OFFICIAL town in the state of Florida!

In addition, the post office connects our town to the rest of the country, welcoming mail and communication with many of our friends and family spread out elsewhere. Many who have not spoken in time can discover one another again, and the Post Office welcomes a new age of collaboration between our townspeople and our neighbors.

If you wish to use the Post Office to send mail or establish your respective mailing slots, please come down anytime this following week and ask for one. This will officially establish your residency and address here in Eatonville, and provide an anchor into our beautiful town!

Although there was some initial opposition, our town no doubt welcomes this new acquisition, and we thank Mayor Starks whole-heartedly for his work in obtaining it.
