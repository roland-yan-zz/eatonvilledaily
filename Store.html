LOCAL NEWS: The Town Store Is Open!

Interested in some quick soul-food, tools, canned goods, candy, toys, oil lamps, or more? Come on down to our new store!

Posted by Local News on September 22, 1887

Do you need a new pair of shoes, dresses, canned food, lamps, tools or anything? Well, the Town Store is open and will be selling anything you need! From tools to home goods, you can buy all you want at cheap prices.

The store will be operating every weekday from dawn to sunset, and will be manned by no other than Mrs.Starks herself!

Just wanting to hang out or try some new food?

The store will also be selling quick snacks and meals every day at noon and late evenings, so hop on by to try some delicious treats and meet some great people! Everyone has a place at the Store!

Eatonville will no doubt be improved by the new store, as it will serve a nexus for town activity and gatherings for friends and neighbors.
