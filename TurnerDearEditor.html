Dear Editors: Improve Your English.

I am here to help improve this sorry excuse of a paper.

Posted by Mrs. Turner on May 3, 1890

Dear Editors, You Are Welcome.

I recently dedicated an hour of my life to peruse the excuse of a newspaper you call the Eatonville Daily. Here I will make short and simple the improvements you need to make in order to retain the attention of lighters viewers such as myself.

The grammatical errors make most articles unreadable. Try hiring writers without any such writing impediments. Furthermore, make articles targeted towards a more refined reader.

I care about home refinement and proper etiquette over squabbling issues of local politics. My advice. Hire some lighter skinned writers, then maybe your paper will have a chance.

Please Improve Your Paper

Sincerely,
Mrs. Turner
