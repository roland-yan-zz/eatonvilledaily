Don’t Forget The Ladies.

The place of ladies, in this world, dominated by men.

Posted by Anonymous on October 12, 1887.

We have stayed silent for the most part, scowling under hidden smiles, caressing with dulled nails, and swaying with tied hair.

With recent events come a new conflict with the places of a woman, as to whether their positions at jobs hold value or degrade from their inefficiencies (a folly I still cannot comprehend the reasoning for). How has a woman’s ability and skill been a barrier to progress and advancement in the place of a man? Without education or training, anybody, man or woman cannot succeed.

There is no negatives to the integrating of women to professional jobs, and great benefits, we can easily reap.

And what do I hope to spread from this? A simple notion.

Don’t forget the ladies.
