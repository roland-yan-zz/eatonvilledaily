What Now?

The people of Eatonville now await, who will now stand where Mayor Starks has fallen?

Posted by Local News on September 26, 1887.

Joe Starks, our great Mayor, fell ill a few weeks back and unfortunately passed away.

He built the very foundation of our town and delivered all he said he would. His leadership dents the very ground of our town and leaves an impression that very few can fill.

Who will step up and become our new great leader, and deliver us all that they can promise? The town of Eatonville is thirsting for growth and ventures, and deserve a man who can plant their roots, fulfilling the promises for our people.

We are waiting. The dawn of a new, hopeful morning encroaches, and the light of a star shall cleanse us again from the darkness.

Who will serve our town, and leave Mayor Starks a legacy that will grant him peace?
