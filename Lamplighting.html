LOCAL NEWS: Lamp-lighting Festival!

Eatonville will be celebrating its new street-lamp tomorrow, midnight in the Town Square.

Posted by Local News on September 12, 1887.

Tomorrow, all of Eatonville will be celebrating the establishment of our very first street lamp!

No longer will be navigating the streets in darkness. No longer shall we fear the shadows of the night. The street lamp will light our way throughout the night, and provide our townspeople the guidance to be productive and safe citizens, throughout all hours of the day.

Once again thanks to Mayor Starks, our town will be revolutionized by the new acquisitions, building ourselves up stronger and better. As a result, we will be celebrating its new establishment in an amazing festival tomorrow evening, gathered around the Town Square.

Take your families, your wife, your children, and even your cousins. We will be serving food, having entertainment, and offering all necessary amenities to have a splendid time.

Interested in volunteering to set up the festival, or contribute your own food? Come on day tomorrow morning, and help us set everything up!

We will see everyone tomorrow!
