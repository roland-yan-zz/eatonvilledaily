May Vergible Woods Rest In Peace.

An obituary and memorial tribute to a doting brother, friend, and husband.

Posted by The Woods Family on August 23, 1890

A Great Loss.

Unfortuhntly, all good tings got tuh come tuh a end. Deah Vergible Woods, or Tea Cake as friends known ‘im, had a good life ‘til he passed way yest’day proteckin his family.

Tea Cake wuhs a treasure soul dat is gonna be missin majorly by all dat lives dat he’s changed. He’s a helped up with tough times for all dem people in da whole town wit his big smile and jolly laugh, he’s a made a house a home, and he saved me twice over.

Tea Cake is survived by da lovin’ community of da Everglades, his wife, and da stories of his fabled life which’ll live on throughout the South.

Tea Cake found the Lord and tought us to find ‘im as well. Share you’se stories of de man dis Sunday at the Eatonville Central Church, and together lets work tuh extend his lovin’ hand as far as we can.

May He Rest In Eternal Peace.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
