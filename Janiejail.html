LOCAL NEWS: Once A Lady, Now Turned Criminal?

Janie Starks, the widowed wife of former Mayor Starks, is facing murder charges and trial.

Posted by Local News on August 29, 1890.

Janie Starks, the former wife of our fallen Mayor, Joe Starks, is now on trial for murder in the Everglades.

Once a shining lady of example, Janie Starks was a role model for all the wives of Eatonville. She ran the Town Store, caring for it with a tender touch and acting as a beacon for the children of the town. She was beloved, and despite running off with the young Tea Cake, was still respected.

However, we only recently heard the shocking news that Mrs.Starks had killed Tea Cake some time after the Hurricane, and will be facing hefty Criminal Charges for murder, down in the Everglades.
